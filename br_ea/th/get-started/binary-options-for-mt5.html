Introduction to binary options on MT5

Binary options are now available for you to trade on MT5 – our advanced multi-asset trading platform that offers unlimited trading possibilities.

Why you should trade binary options on MT5

  • Trade binary options and CFDs from the same powerful interface
  • Use MT5’s comprehensive analysis tools for technical and fundamental analysis to predict price movement
  • Enjoy real-time visualisation and trading history with our Expert Advisor for binary options trading

ตลาดที่เปิดให้บริการ

Binary options on MT5 are available exclusively on Volatility Indices which are synthetic indices that simulate market volatility and are available for trading 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Five total assets are available for you to choose from:

  • Volatility 10 Index
  • Volatility 25 Index
  • Volatility 50 Index
  • Volatility 75 Index
  • Volatility 100 Index

Each index corresponds to simulated markets with constant volatilities of 10%, 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% respectively.

ประเภทของการซื้อขาย

Execute your trading strategy using the Rise/Fall trade type with a duration of five or 10 ticks. With Rise/Fall contracts, you must predict whether the market will rise or fall from its current level.

How to trade binary options on MT5

Get started with the Binary.com Expert Advisor (EA) for binary options trading on MT5 by following these steps:

1

Add the Binary.com EA to MT5

2

Enable automated trading

3

Start trading binary options on MT5

Step 1: Add the Binary.com EA to MT5

Firstly, log in to your MT5 Volatility Indices account.

Next, proceed to download the Binary.com EA for binary options trading on MT5 and save the file to your desktop.

After that, simply double-click the file to add the EA to your MT5 platform.

Step 2: Enable automated trading

Now, open your MT5 platform and head over to the Navigator window to see if the Binary.com EA is available.

Then, select any of the available symbols to open a live chart.

After the live chart is active, double-click on the Binary.com EA in the Navigator window.

A pop-up window will appear and you will need to check the box labelled 'Allow Automated Trading'.

If automated trading is enabled, you should see the 'Auto Trading' indicator turn green.

Step 3: Start trading

Now you can buy your first binary option contract on MT5.

To get started, select a symbol. Then define your payout and duration.

Finally, make your prediction: will the market price end up higher or lower than the entry spot at the end of the contract?

If you need to review a previous transaction, you may browse your full transaction history via the 'History' tab.
